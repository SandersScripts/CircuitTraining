Circuit-Training

Lessons in coding, circuits, and problem solving

What I can do for you

Is your child interested in computers or would you like to introduce coding concepts to your son or daughter but you don't know where to begin? Is camp over, and now you want your children to learn valuable skills while having fun? Do you want to work out your child's brain on a summer rainy day?
I am here to help. As an honors, accelerated robotics, AP computer science student, and published Minecraft designer, I can teach children ages 5-10 a variety of introductory and challenging computer and electronics skills:
  • -MIT's Scratch Creative Computing
  • -Minecraft Redstone
  • -Gamemaker
  • -Basic HTML, Java, Python, etc
  • -Basic Circuitry
  • -More advanced coding principles upon request

About Myself

I have been interested in robotics, engineering and computers since my earliest memories. I continue to be an avid Lego builder, and recently my work as a part of a Minecraft building team has been published in a book recommended by the Wall Street Journal. I am self-taught in a variety of computer languages such as Python, Java, JavaScript, and HTML. I am an active member of the Millburn High School computer science and State ranked robotics clubs. I enjoy sharing my love of computers and electronics with others, and I hope to instill my passion for skills that are ever increasing in demand in today's technologically oriented world.

Availability

During August I am available most days, seven days a week. I often do volunteer work in a local Assisted community a few hours a week alongside work in my family business, but I have flexibility with my schedule. During the school year, I work around my robotics and computer club, as well as fencing team, schedules.
Initially, I meet with you and/or your child in person or Skype so you can get to know me and I can learn more about your son or daughter's abilities and interest. There is no charge for the 10-15 minute consultation. My hourly rate is $15 and I generally find that kids have a hard time focusing beyond an hour, but I am willing, if time and interest allows, to conduct longer sessions.
I exclusively service the Millburn, Short Hills and Maplewood New Jersey communities.
